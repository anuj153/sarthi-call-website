Is Sarthi an AI or a real advisor?

Sarthi combines the speed of AI with the expertise of SEBI-registered professionals. AI helps identify opportunities, while every trade call is backed by our expert research team.

How is Sarthi different from other trading tools?

Sarthi combines AI with SEBI-registered experts to deliver timely trade calls, market insights, and guidance—helping you make informed trading decisions with confidence.

How often will Sarthi call me?

You decide how often Sarthi reaches out. Choose your preferred call frequency—from 1–2 calls, 3–5 calls, or all trade calls—anytime from Settings.

Can I turn off Sarthi calls or alerts?

Yes. You can manage your communication preferences anytime from the app settings or by contacting our support team.

What can I ask Sarthi?

You can ask Sarthi about trade ideas, market trends, risk management, portfolio-related queries, and how to make the most of your trading opportunities.
